Download Dual Tone Image Template

Make your own dual tone images, download file here.

To have this effect on your images you need first, desaturate (or make it black and white) your image then place the filters that comes in the psd file above your image.

1. Open your image on photoshop

2. Go to Images» Adjustments» Desaturate

3. Place the filters above your desaturated image

Done!
